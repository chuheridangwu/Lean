MAN: Give me a book please, Jane.
WOMAN: Which book?
WOMAN: This one?
MAN: No, not that one. The red one.
WOMAN: This one?
MAN: Yes, please.
WOMAN: Here you are.
MAN: Thank you.

丈夫：请拿本书给我，简。
